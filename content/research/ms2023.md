#### Abstract:

The presence of an occasionally binding constraint due to the zero lower bound (ZLB) in New Keynesian models generally gives rise to multiple equilibria under active monetary policy. To restore uniqueness in the model with an active Taylor Rule, we consider appropriate simple fiscal policy instruments. Without relaxing the assumptions of full information and rational expectations, we show that appropriate fiscal targeting rules ensure that New Keynesian models subject to the ZLB possess a unique solution.
